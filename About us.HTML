Royal Kebab House in Macquarie Fields is the place to go for tasty pizza and kebabs. Select from three sizes of kebab meals featuring beef kebab roll, chicken kebab roll or mixed kebab roll. Several sizes of snack packs are available for all three as well. Vegetarians may enjoy a falafel roll or plate or a special veggie plate entree. Turkish pide, traditional pizzas, gourmet pizzas and burgers are sure to please patrons who might not be in the mood for kebabs. Sides, salads and soft drinks are available to round out the meal.
